ODIN RECIPES:Milkshake

A lovely treat for the whole family, to share with friends or a cozy night in.

Easy recipe for beginners.

Ingredients
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • 1 banana, finely chopped
  • chocolate sauce
Method
  1. Blend milk, banana and ice for 1 minute.
  2. Add chocolate sauce to an empty glass, smearing it on the sides.
  3. Pour out blended ingredients into glass and enjoy.
