ODIN RECIPES:Burger

A lovely treat for the whole family, to share with friends or a cozy night in.

Easy recipe for beginners.

Ingredients
  • Cheese slices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • San Remo Burger Patty
  • Lettuce, fine chopped
  • Buns
Method
  1. Fry the patty in olive oil for 7-10 minutes. Then take it out and leave to rest
  2. Slice the bun and toast with cheese for 30-50 seconds.
  3. To assemble the burger, add patty, lettuce and ketchup to taste.
